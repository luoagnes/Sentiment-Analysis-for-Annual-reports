during the year under review, the significant increase in oil price and raw material price imposed tremendous pressure on different industries in the global market. as similar to other manufacturers in the industry, the group was also inevitably amidst such challenging business environment the group introduced a number of effective measures to minimize the adverse impact brought forth by the upsurge in raw material prices. in addition to transferring the increase in cost to customers, the group also further enhanced its vertical integration model while implementing stringent cost controls.
