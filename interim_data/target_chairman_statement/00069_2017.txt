on behalf of the board, i would like to present the annual report of shangri-la asia limited for the financial year ended 31 december 2016.
the group achieved improved hotel performance in mainland china and southeast asia despite the challenging global and regional business environment and the impact of weakening local currencies on hotels operating performance. the group also opened three hotels, including our first property in sri lanka, and signed a management agreement for one hotel being developed by a third party.
the performance of our investment property portfolio again recorded a healthy improvement. property sales increased in 2016 mainly from residential sales in mainland china, and property rentals and sales made a positive contribution to the groups results for the year.
consolidated sales revenue decreased slightly by 3% over 2015 to us$2,055.4 million. after tax profit (before non-operating items) attributable to shareholders increased by 60% compared to the previous year. after tax profit (inclusive of non-operating items) attributable to shareholders decreased by 24% to us$106.1 million.
