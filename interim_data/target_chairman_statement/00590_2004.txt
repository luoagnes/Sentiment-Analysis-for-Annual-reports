chairman s statement wong wai sheung, the chairman i am pleased to present the annual report of luk fook holdings (international) limited (the company ) and its subsidiaries (collectively known as the group ) for the year ended 31st march for the year ended 31st march 2004, turnover was hk$1,598,123,000, representing a 9.3% increase compared with hk$1,461,763,000 in the previous year. profit attributable to shareholders amounted to about hk$76,965,000 (2003: hk$47,357,000), representing an increase of 62.5%. earnings per share was hk16.1 cents (2003: hk10.0 cents).

the directors proposed a final dividend of hk5 cents per share (2003: hk4 cents per share) for the year ended 31st march 2004.
together with the interim dividend of hk2 cents per share already paid, a total of hk7 cents per share were declared for the year ended 31st march 2004 (2003: hk6 cents per share).
